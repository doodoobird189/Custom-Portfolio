About Me!

I was born on the 18th of September 2002 in Harare, Zimbabwe. My family moved out of Zimbabwe when he was very young in search of a more stable, safer country. Having lived in more than 3 african countries, I grew to appreciate other cultures. I developed hobbies such as skating and playing chess while living in Nairobi, Kenya and developed and interest in programming in highschool in South Africa. I am now learning Javascript at Code College in an attempt to further my programming knowledge and perhaps pursue a career in software development.
